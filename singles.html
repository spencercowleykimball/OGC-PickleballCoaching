Why Singles?

Singles sharpens every weakness doubles lets you hide. You cover the whole court — footwork, endurance, and shot construction all improve faster. It also builds doubles IQ you can't get any other way.

Singles vs. Doubles — Key Differences

Serving strategy, middle coverage, speed-up frequency, kitchen time — all shift dramatically in singles. We'll often debrief as a group after games to discuss what's working tactically.
